Fundraising lead time benchmarks from Bridge Span Group 2023 Impact Investing Report and NCVO UK Civil Society Almanac 2024. Reserve recommendations from NCVO Financial Resilience Framework. Reading the timeline: Red cells indicate months where cash drops below your reserve target. Base scenario uses confirmed + probable income at 70% certainty. Pessimistic uses confirmed income only.
Three scenarios modelled using income certainty levels and cost variance benchmarks from the NCVO Financial Resilience Framework. Pessimistic applies 15% cost overrun (the sector median for unexpected cost increases).
