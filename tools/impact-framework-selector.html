Why this tool exists

There are 30+ impact measurement frameworks. Most are mutually compatible, a few overlap significantly, and each one is optimised for a specific purpose. Choosing wrong means wasted effort, poor reporting, and missed funding. This tool matches your organisation's context against 20 major frameworks and recommends the combination that fits. Based on GIIN's 2023 Annual Impact Investor Survey (N=308), IMM Practices Report 2020, UNDP SDG Impact Standards (2021), and the Bridgespan / Rockefeller Philanthropy Advisors Impact Management landscape review.

Implementation Roadmap

A phased 12-month plan to stand up your recommended framework stack. Tailored to your current maturity level and capacity.

Why phased: Organisations that try to implement multiple frameworks simultaneously typically fail within 18 months (GIIN IMM Practices Report 2020). The most successful approach is primary framework first (months 1-6), then layer complementary frameworks (months 7-12).
