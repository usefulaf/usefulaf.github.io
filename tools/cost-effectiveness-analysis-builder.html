🧪 Ingredient Costing Table WFP/3ie Standard
Methodology: Ingredient costing (WFP/3ie/USAID standard) identifies every resource consumed and assigns a unit cost. Include all ingredients: staff time at opportunity cost, materials, overhead, training, monitoring. Do not undercount — undercounting is the most common CEA error.
